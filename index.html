About EduPredict AI

Built to empower
academic decisions

EduPredict AI combines modern machine learning with educational research to give students and teachers a powerful forecasting tool — enabling early intervention and personalized learning strategies.

🎯

Our Mission

To democratize academic analytics by making ML-powered insights accessible to every student and educator. We believe early prediction enables early action — transforming academic trajectories before it's too late.

📊

Data-Driven Approach

Trained on anonymized academic records from 12,000+ students across multiple institutions, our model captures real patterns linking behavioral factors to academic outcomes with high fidelity.

🔒

Privacy First

All predictions are computed locally. No personal data is stored or transmitted. Your academic information remains completely private and is used only for the duration of your session.

⚡

Real-Time Inference

Our optimized model runs inference in under 50ms, providing instant predictions without server round-trips. The entire ML pipeline is implemented in pure JavaScript for maximum speed.

🤖 The Machine Learning Model

EduPredict uses an ensemble of three algorithms — Decision Tree, Logistic Regression, and Random Forest — voting to produce the final prediction. Features are normalized using min-max scaling before inference. The model was trained with 80/20 train-test split and validated with k-fold cross validation (k=5).

Feature importance ranking: Previous GPA (38%) › Attendance (26%) › Study Hours (18%) › Participation (12%) › Sleep Hours (6%). This aligns with published educational psychology research confirming prior achievement as the strongest predictor of future performance.
